About

I'm a lifelong technology enthusiast with a deep interest in how things work under the hood. My background isn't formal — no CS degree, no certifications — but I've spent years learning by doing, and I'm convinced that's the best way to actually understand something.

Right now I'm slowly working through the fundamentals of programming — not just the syntax, but the way of thinking that lets you look at a problem and know how to approach building a solution. It's a long road, but the goal is clear: to eventually be able to write software that's genuinely useful to someone.

When I'm not at a keyboard, I'm usually deep in some other rabbit hole — reconfiguring a homeserver, automating something around the house, or gaming.
